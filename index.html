Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number "Your Target Number" at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the target number. Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
